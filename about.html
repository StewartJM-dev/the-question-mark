About

The show, and who's asking

Gordon Conger hosts The Question Mark Podcast, part of the LWBC Media Ministry out of Living Word Baptist Church in Sweet Valley, PA. He also designed LWBC's logo and runs the church's social media.

The Question Mark is more than a podcast—it's an invitation to sit with the questions that matter most. Hosted by Gordon Conger through LWBC Let's Connect, each episode takes a single, probing question and holds it up to the light of Biblical truth.

This is the space where we ask the hard questions—about faith, identity, purpose, prayer, service, and the nature of God. Not to arrive at easy answers, but to go deeper. To search. To grow. To be honest about what we don't know, and courageous about what we believe.

Every episode is a journey from question to insight—from the uncertainty of the question mark to the conviction of the answer. We believe that the bravest thing you can do is ask.
