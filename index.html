Welcome to the Question Mark

The Question Mark Podcast sits with the parts of faith that don’t resolve easily — and takes them seriously anyway. Hosted by Gordon Conger, the show is part of the media ministry of Living Word Baptist Church in Sweet Valley, Pennsylvania. This is a place for the questions that linger after the sermon ends, the ones that surface at 2 a.m., the ones that feel too big or too honest to say out loud in a pew. Gordon brings each of them into the open with care, humility, and an unwavering conviction that God’s Word has something real to say — not to silence the question, but to meet it.

Around here, a hard question isn’t a threat to faith — it’s an invitation to open the Book and look closer. Whether you are a lifelong believer who has hit a wall, someone who is just beginning to explore what Christianity is all about, or anywhere in between, you are welcome at this table. Each episode is a conversation rooted in Scripture, shaped by honesty, and offered with the belief that asking is the first step toward finding. So pull up a chair, bring whatever is weighing on you, pick a door below, and come on in.

Add to your home screen

The Question Mark isn’t in the App Store — but your phone can install it straight from this site, with its own icon and no browser bar, in a few taps.

  1. Tap the Share button (the square with an arrow pointing up) in Safari’s toolbar.
  2. Scroll down and tap “Add to Home Screen.”
  3. Tap “Add” in the top right corner.
  1. Tap the ⋮ menu (three dots) in Chrome’s toolbar — or tap “Install” directly if Chrome offers it.
  2. Tap “Add to Home screen” or “Install app.”
  3. Confirm by tapping “Add” or “Install.”

Once added, the icon opens the site full-screen, just like a downloaded app.
