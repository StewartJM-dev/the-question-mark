The Question Mark Podcast

A ministry of Living Word Baptist Church · Sweet Valley, PA

What if the question matters more than the answer?

Welcome. The Question Mark Podcast sits with the parts of faith that don’t resolve easily — and takes them seriously anyway. Hosted by Gordon Conger, the show is part of the media ministry of Living Word Baptist Church in Sweet Valley, Pennsylvania.

Around here, a hard question isn’t a threat to faith — it’s an invitation to open the Book and look closer. Pick a door below and come on in.
